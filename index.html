About the app

We are a group of students with a common with a common mission to help India during the Covid crisis, brought us all together to create this platform. Here we ask our users from drurgapur or near durgapur to upload any kind of valuable resources they are aware of, and will also be useful for a patient. 

Job Of Volunteers ?

We are sure that you might be wondering about the job of a volunteer? Right? But wait for a minute. In this platform we try to connect people away from the tradional methods of mentorship. Here the volunteers need to verify the data sublitted by the users by calling or chatting through an  simple app. Which will be exclusively available to the volunteers only. Currently we are serving Durgapur as our prime location, but later we will increase our reach with more number of volunteers abroad. Its our rquest from the coviware team, while verifying the data, we should provide anough respect to the resource provider and supplier. As they serve as the Frontline Warriors.

How To Verify ?

As we have mentioned earlier, our volunteers will have an exlusive app to deal with data verification. The volunteer  will have two options "Call" & "Chat", he/she can use any of the ways to have a conversation with the resource supplier. Now you might be thinking of what are the things to ask while having a conversation with the supplier?

  • Verify the name of his shop/service as submitted by an user.
  • Verify the category of resource he/she provides as mentioned by the user.
  • Verify if the supplier really wants to display his/her service details in our app.
  • If the all the data's match as of submitted by the user, then the volunteer will have to click on the "Take Action Button" to publish the data.
  • If the data's didnt match or the supplier didn't want to share the details then skip that particular data and move on to the next one.
